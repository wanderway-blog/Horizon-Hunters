About Asian Temples

Asian temples offer spaces of contemplation and beauty. From the zen gardens of Kyoto to the rice terraces of Bali, these sacred spaces connect travelers to centuries of tradition and peaceful energy.

This gallery captures the stillness and detail that makes temple visits so meaningful - morning light on wooden structures, carefully raked gravel gardens, and the quiet presence of historic shrines.

Explore the Kyoto Morning guide to learn how to experience these sacred spaces at their most peaceful.
