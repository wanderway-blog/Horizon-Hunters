Maldives

Welcome to the Maldives

The Maldives is a beautiful country made of many small islands. It has clear blue water, coral reefs, and nice resorts.

Why Visit the Maldives?

Crystal Clear Water

The water is so clear you can see fish swimming even from above. It looks like a giant swimming pool.

White Sandy Beaches

The beaches have soft white sand. It feels nice to walk on and is perfect for lying down to relax.

Amazing Marine Life

You can see colorful fish, turtles, and even sharks. Snorkeling and diving are very popular here.

What to Do in the Maldives

Swimming

The water is warm all year round. Swimming here is safe and fun for everyone.

Snorkeling

Put on a mask and jump in! You will see beautiful coral reefs and many colorful fish.

Diving

If you have a diving license, explore deeper waters. If not, you can take lessons at many resorts.

Watch the Sunset

The sunsets in the Maldives are beautiful. The sky turns orange, pink, and purple.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

The best time is from November to April. The weather is dry and sunny.

How Long to Stay

We recommend 5 to 7 nights. This gives you enough time to relax and enjoy all the activities.

What to Pack

Bring swimwear, sunscreen, sunglasses, and light clothes. Don't forget your camera!
