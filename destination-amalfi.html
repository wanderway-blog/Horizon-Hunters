Amalfi Coast, Italy

Welcome to the Amalfi Coast

The Amalfi Coast is a beautiful stretch of coastline in southern Italy. It is famous for its colorful buildings, narrow streets, delicious food, and amazing sea views.

Why Visit the Amalfi Coast?

Stunning Views

Every turn on the road shows you beautiful views of the sea and colorful buildings.

Delicious Italian Food

Eat fresh seafood, homemade pasta, and local lemons. The food here is simple and very tasty.

Charming Towns

Visit Positano, Amalfi, and Ravello. Each town has its own character and beautiful streets.

Top Places to Visit

Positano

A pretty town with colorful houses going down to the beach. Great for shopping.

Amalfi

The main town with a beautiful cathedral and a busy main square.

Ravello

A quiet town up in the hills with amazing views. Famous for its beautiful gardens.

Capri

A nearby island you can visit by boat. It has caves and lovely shops.

Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Visit from April to June or September to October. The weather is nice and there are fewer tourists.

How to Travel

You can take a bus along the coast or drive. Ferries are also a nice way to travel between towns.

What to Bring

Wear comfortable shoes for walking on steep streets. Bring sunscreen and a hat!
